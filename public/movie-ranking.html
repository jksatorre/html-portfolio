The best kdrama according to Jester

My top 3 K-dramas of all time

Descendant of the sun

I loved the cinematography, the chemistry of the leads, the second-leads’ love story, the action sequences and the story in general.

Itaewon Class

I do think it’s a good first K-drama to watch, as it has a really interesting plot.

Queen of tears

I really loved the drama as I feel it taught several poignant lessons about marriage, and in some ways offered a lovely reflection of what a biblical marriage should be.
